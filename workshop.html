Since 2023, I have co-organized the annual Social Cost of Water Pollution Workshop in collaboration with the Cornell Atkinson Center for Sustainability and the U.S. EPA. This event serves as a forum for economists, policy experts, engineers, and other professionals to discuss Integrated Assessment Models (IAMs) for water quality.

About the Conference: The workshop is designed to encourage research and policy dialogue on IAMs for water quality.
