Social Cost of Water Pollution Workshop

I am part of a team organizing the annual Social Cost of Water Pollution Workshop since 2023. The Cornell Atkinson Center for Sustainability and the U.S. EPA are partnering with us to make this event happen.

Our main goal is to provide a space where economists, public policy experts, engineers, and other interested professionals can come together and share their work on Integrated Assessment Modeling (IAMs) for water pollution.
