Since 2023, I have co-organized the annual Social Cost of Water Pollution Workshop. This initiative is a joint effort with Professors David Keiser (UMass Amherst), Cathy Kling (Cornell), Dan Phaneuf (University of Wisconsin-Madison), and Tina Andarge (UMass Amherst), in collaboration with the Cornell Atkinson Center for Sustainability and the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA). The workshop serves as a platform for economists, policy experts, engineers, and other professionals to discuss Integrated Assessment Models (IAMs) for water quality.

The event is designed to foster interdisciplinary collaboration and policy dialogue on IAMs for water quality.
