Social Cost of Water Pollution Workshop

I have been co-organizing the annual Social Cost of Water Pollution Workshop since 2023. This event is held in collaboration with the Cornell Atkinson Center for Sustainability and the U.S. EPA.

The workshop provides a forum for interdisciplinary dialogue among economists, policy experts, engineers, and other professionals. Our aim is to advance research and policy discussions on Integrated Assessment Models (IAMs) for water quality and to evaluate the benefits and costs of water quality improvements.

About the Conference: This workshop is designed to coordinate and stimulate research and policy discussions on IAMs for water quality. It seeks to build a strong interdisciplinary community focused on understanding how changes in water quality affect society, with the ultimate goal of providing regional and temporal estimates of the social cost of water pollution.
