Welcome

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Department of Resource Economics at UMass Amherst. Starting in Fall 2025, I will join Indiana University Bloomington as an Assistant Professor. My research focuses on environmental and natural resource economics—with an emphasis on land use, water resources, and public policy.

I hold a Ph.D. in Natural Resource Economics and Environmental Informatics from Virginia Tech, along with degrees in Development Economics (Brazil) and Mechanical Engineering (India). My work examines the interplay between human and environmental systems and develops Integrated Assessment Models (IAMs) to tackle water quality challenges, particularly nutrient pollution.
