Welcome

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Department of Resource Economics at UMass Amherst. Starting Fall 2025, I will join Indiana University Bloomington as an Assistant Professor. My work focuses on environmental and natural resource economics with special attention to land use, water resources, and public policy.

I hold a Ph.D. in Natural Resource Economics and Environmental Informatics from Virginia Tech, USA, along with degrees in Development Economics from Brazil and Mechanical Engineering from India. My research examines the interactions between human and environmental systems—focusing on the economic and health impacts of land and water policies—and I develop Integrated Assessment Models (IAMs) to address water quality challenges, particularly nutrient pollution.
