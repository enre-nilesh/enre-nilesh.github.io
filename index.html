Welcome

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Associate at UMass Amherst and will join Indiana University Bloomington as an Assistant Professor in Fall 2025. My research focuses on environmental and natural resource economics—with special emphasis on land use, water resources, and public policy.

I earned a Ph.D. in Natural Resource Economics and Environmental Informatics from Virginia Tech, and I also hold degrees in Development Economics (Brazil) and Mechanical Engineering (India). My work investigates the interactions between human and environmental systems and develops Integrated Assessment Models (IAMs) to address water quality challenges, particularly nutrient pollution.
