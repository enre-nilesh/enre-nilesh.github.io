Welcome to My Website

Hello, I'm Nilesh SN Shinde. I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Department of Resource Economics at UMass Amherst, and starting Fall 2025 I will join Indiana University Bloomington as an Assistant Professor. My work focuses on environmental and natural resource economics with special attention to land use, water resources, and public policy.

Introduction

I hold a Ph.D. in Natural Resource Economics and Environmental Informatics from Virginia Tech, with additional degrees from Brazil and India. My research explores how environmental policies affect economic outcomes, and I work to develop Integrated Assessment Models (IAMs) to study water quality and related issues.
