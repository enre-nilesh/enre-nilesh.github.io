Welcome to My Website

Hello, I'm Nilesh Shinde. I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Department of Resource Economics at the University of Massachusetts Amherst, and in Fall 2025 I will join Indiana University Bloomington as an Assistant Professor in the O’Neill School of Public and Environmental Affairs. My work focuses on environmental and natural resource economics with particular attention to land use, water resources, and their impact on human health and policy.

Introduction

I hold a Ph.D. in Natural Resource Economics and Environmental Informatics from Virginia Tech, USA, along with degrees in Development Economics from Brazil and Mechanical Engineering from India. My research examines the interactions between human and environmental systems and is dedicated to quantifying the economic and health impacts of land and water policies. I also work on developing Integrated Assessment Models (IAMs) to address water quality challenges, particularly nutrient pollution.

Please explore my publications, working papers, and resources to learn more about my research and ongoing projects.
