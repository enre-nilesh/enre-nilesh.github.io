About Me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Department of Resource Economics at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. Starting Fall 2025, I will join the O’Neill School of Public and Environmental Affairs (SPEA) at Indiana University Bloomington as an Assistant Professor.

My research focuses on how human and environmental systems interact, with special attention to health, land use, and water resources. I aim to:

  • Evaluate the economic and health impacts of land and water policies.
  • Develop Integrated Assessment Models (IAMs) to address water quality challenges, particularly nutrient pollution.

I earned a Ph.D. in Natural Resource Economics and Environmental Informatics from Virginia Tech, and also hold degrees in Development Economics (Brazil) and Mechanical Engineering (India).
