Published Papers

  • “The Cold, Hard Truth: Evidence from Heterogeneous Snowpack Insulation Effect on Winter Wheat Yields in China”
    Accepted for Environment and Development Economics (2025) with Zhenyu Yao. Click to view the paper details and DOI information.
  • "The Human Well-Being Potential of Tree Plantations in Sub-Saharan Africa: A Reassessment of Evidence Using Longitudinal Subnational-Year Data"
    with Edouard Mensah, Ange Kakpo, and Ida Djenontin in Forest Policy and Economics, Volume 160 (2024): 103141. Explore the findings on tree plantations and human well-being.
  • "Collective Property Rights Lead to Secondary Forest Growth in the Brazilian Amazon"
    with Kathryn Baragwanath and Ella Bayi in Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences, 120, No. 22 (2023): e2221346120. Awarded the 5th edition of the MapBiomas Award; featured in Mongabay and the World Economic Forum.
  • "Incentives for Rural Households to Establish Tree Cover on Agricultural Land in Andhra Pradesh"
    with Stella Schons, Greg Amacher, Kelly Cobourn, and Haripirya Gundimeda in Environmental Economics in Developing Countries: Issues and Challenges (2022): 154. Investigates rural incentives for environmental improvement.

Land

    Journal of Environmental Economics and Management (Invited for Special Issue) with Kathryn Baragwanath. Assessing satellite data use in deforestation policy evaluation.
